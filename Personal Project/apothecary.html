Apothecary

Sacred healers preserving the future of the Legion.

Guardians of Life and Legacy

Apothecaries carry the sacred duty of preserving the Blood Angels’ gene-seed — the essence of the Legion’s future. Their battlefield role transcends healing, ensuring the survival of their brothers for generations.

Masters of War Medicine

Armed with ancient surgical knowledge and advanced medicae technology, Apothecaries stabilize grievous wounds and extract gene-seed from fallen warriors amidst the chaos of battle.

The Sacred Ritual

Each gene-seed harvest is a solemn ritual, as Apothecaries honor the fallen and safeguard the bloodline that sustains the Chapter’s warriors.
