Assault Marine

Swift shock troops, masters of close combat.

Speed and Ferocity

Assault Marines specialize in rapid insertion and brutal close-quarters combat. Equipped with jump packs, they can quickly close distances, striking deep into enemy lines before foes can react.

Weapons of Vengeance

Armed with chainswords, pistols, and other close combat weapons, Assault Marines embody the Blood Angels' legendary martial prowess. Their ferocity is unmatched, and their attacks leave devastation in their wake.

The Storm of Vengeance

Moving like a storm, Assault Marines sow chaos and fear, disrupting enemy formations and creating openings for their battle-brothers to exploit.
