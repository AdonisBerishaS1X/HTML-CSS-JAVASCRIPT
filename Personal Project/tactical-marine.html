Tactical Marine

Versatile warriors, backbone of the Blood Angels battle line.

Reliable and Adaptable

Tactical Marines are the disciplined, versatile core of the Blood Angels Legion. Trained extensively in a variety of battlefield roles, they can respond to rapidly changing combat situations with precision and flexibility.

Mastery of Arms and Tactics

Armed with bolters and equipped with an array of specialized wargear, Tactical Marines excel in both offense and defense. Their training enables them to hold critical objectives or spearhead assaults, adapting their tactics to any foe.

Unwavering Resolve

As the backbone of the Legions fighting force, Tactical Marines embody the discipline and honor of the Blood Angels. Their steadfastness under fire inspires those around them and turns the tide of battle time and again.
