Inceptor

Lightning-fast assault troopers, delivering crushing firepower.

Rapid Deployment

Inceptors are elite jump-pack equipped troopers, capable of teleporting or rapidly deploying into the thick of battle via drop pods or swift flight.

Heavy Bolt Weapons

Armed with heavy bolt pistols and assault bolters, Inceptors combine speed with formidable firepower, delivering devastating strikes before the enemy can react.

Shock and Awe

Their sudden and relentless assaults sow chaos in enemy ranks, creating critical openings and supporting broader Blood Angels offensive maneuvers.
