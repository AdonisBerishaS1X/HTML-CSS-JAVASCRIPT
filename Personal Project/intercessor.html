Intercessor

The disciplined infantry core of the Blood Angels Legion.

Versatile Infantry

Intercessors are the backbone of the Blood Angels’ infantry. Armed with powerful bolt rifles, they provide both offense and defense with precision and discipline.

Hold and Push

Whether holding critical ground or pressing relentless attacks, Intercessors excel at adapting to battlefield conditions with steadfast resolve.

Endurance and Precision

Trained for endurance and marksmanship, they can withstand brutal engagements and maintain fire superiority over extended battles.
