Sanguinary Guard

Heavenly warriors clad in gold, guardians of legacy and honor.

Legacy of Sanguinius

The Sanguinary Guard are the spiritual descendants of the Primarch Sanguinius, chosen for their purity, valor, and skill. They once formed his personal retinue and now serve as elite protectors of the Chapter Master. They are warriors, saints, and symbols of the Chapter's hope.

Golden Angels of Death

Adorned in ornate golden armor and wielding encarmine blades and angelus-pattern boltguns, the Sanguinary Guard descend into battle like vengeful seraphim. Their angelic visages and wings strike fear into the heretic and inspiration into the hearts of loyalists.

The Burden of Glory

To be among the Guard is the highest honor — but also a crushing responsibility. They carry the memory of Sanguinius into every battle, expected to fight with perfection and fall with grace.
