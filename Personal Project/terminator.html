Terminator

Unstoppable juggernauts clad in nearly impenetrable armor.

Heavy Armor Titans

Terminators wear the iconic Tactical Dreadnought Armor, offering unparalleled protection on the battlefield. They are the Legion’s most resilient and powerful warriors.

Shock Assault Specialists

Armed with thunder hammers, storm bolters, and other heavy weapons, Terminators lead crushing assaults that break enemy lines with overwhelming force.

Fearless Vanguard

Their imposing presence inspires allies and terrifies foes as they relentlessly push forward in the fiercest combat zones.
